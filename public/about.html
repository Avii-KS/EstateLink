About EstateLink

Transforming Property Management in India

Our Mission

To create a unified, transparent, and efficient property management system that serves the citizens of India by providing easy access to property records and related services.

Key Features

Unified Search

Search across multiple government databases including DORIS, DLR, CERSAI, and MCA21

Secure Access

Advanced security measures to protect your property information

Digital Records

Access property records and documents online anytime, anywhere

Transparent Transactions

Clear visibility into property ownership and encumbrances

Our Journey

2020

Project Inception

EstateLink was conceptualized to modernize property management in India

2021

Development Phase

Integration with existing government databases began

2022

Pilot Launch

Initial testing in select states with positive feedback

2023

National Rollout

System made available to all citizens across India
