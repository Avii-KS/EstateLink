What is an Encumbrances Certificate?

An Encumbrances Certificate (EC) is a legal document that provides information about any legal liabilities or charges on a property.

Processing Time

Standard processing time is 3-5 working days. Express service available for urgent requests.

Required Documents

  • Property ID proof
  • Owner's identity proof
  • Address proof
  • Previous EC (if any)

Frequently Asked Questions

What information does an EC contain?

An EC contains details of all registered transactions related to the property, including sales, mortgages, leases, and other encumbrances.

How long is an EC valid?

An EC is valid for 6 months from the date of issue. For legal purposes, a fresh EC may be required.

Can I get an EC for any property?

Yes, you can request an EC for any registered property in India. The property must be properly registered with the government.
