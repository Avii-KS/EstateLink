Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a property?

Property registration can be done through our online portal. You'll need to provide property details, ownership documents, and pay the required fees. Our step-by-step guide will help you through the process.

What documents are required for property search?

For property search, you'll need either the property ID, owner's name, or address details. Additional documents may be required depending on the type of search you're conducting.

How can I get an encumbrance certificate?

You can apply for an encumbrance certificate online through our portal. The process requires property details and a nominal fee. The certificate will be issued within 3-5 working days.
